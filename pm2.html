2) How do I prep for PM interviews???

PM interviews are usually multipart, multifaceted processes that may feel challenging. Here are some excellent resources which will prep you well for PM interview processes!

Product School Repo
Website that documents a huge list of the most common product manager interview questions (of different styles and contexts)
View resource
thepminterview.com
Awesome site where you can practice various types of mock-pm-interviews with yourself
View resource
Decode and Conquer
Excellent book that has relevant practice PM interview questions (and detailed sample answers) as well as product interview frameworks (that work)
View resource
Exponent YouTube channel
YouTube channel that has a ton of videos and playlists that demonstrate high quality PM interview question responses and demos. I personally really liked the "Improve Spotify's Social Features" practice video.
View resource
Medium article about STAR method
Great article about how to use STAR method to approach behavioral PM interview questions
View resource
